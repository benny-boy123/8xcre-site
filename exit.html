The promise

Sell at the number the math supports.

When it opens, 8x Exit will start with an honest value range — what a business would actually bring in today's market — and only go to market when the number and the buyer are both right. No lowballs. No strip-and-flip. No list-and-pray.

The business industry never taught owners what their company is really worth. Closing that gap is what 8x Exit is being built to do.
