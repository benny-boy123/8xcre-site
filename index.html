The 8x difference

The deal other brokers couldn't structure.

Most brokers list a price and wait. When buyer and seller are apart, the deal dies. We close the gap — with structures that give both sides what they actually need.

Selling a business? We can sell the real estate under it too — or lease it back so you keep the income. Buyer short on cash? Seller financing. Price apart? An earn-out or a phased close. The structure is the deal.
