Who you'll work with

Benny Plaksin

I'm a Las Vegas real estate professional. It started with parks — RV parks and manufactured-housing communities across Nevada — because I kept seeing the same thing: owners buried in lowball letters from people who'd never set foot on the property, and almost nobody giving them an honest, documented answer about what they owned.

8x CRE grew out of that. Same discipline, wider scope: commercial real estate, land, and entitlement across Nevada — and soon, through 8x Exit, the businesses that operate on it. Every conversation confidential. Every number supported. And I walk the property myself.

What 8x CRE stands for

The standard, in plain terms.

01

Honest before flattering

The valuation reflects today's market — not the number that wins us a listing. If it's worth less than you hoped, you hear it from us first.

02

Quiet by default

Off-market to verified buyers under confidentiality. No sign at the entrance, no blast to strangers, no word to your team until you say so.

03

Specialist, not generalist

A short list of asset classes and verticals we know deeply — enough to defend every number we put in writing.

04

On your timeline

Most owners we talk to aren't selling this year. Some never will. The number is yours to plan around, with no follow-up campaign.

05

Nevada-licensed

A licensed Nevada real estate professional. Compliant by default — and clear about the line between a market valuation and a certified appraisal.

06

Fair fees

We're paid a commission only if you choose to sell and we represent you. The valuation is free — that's how we introduce ourselves.
